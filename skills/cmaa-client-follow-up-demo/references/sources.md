## Approved demo stories

**Chicago Flavas — APEX**

CMAA's 2024 Impact Report says the catering business secured its first government contract, worth $50,000, within four months of working with the APEX Accelerator. Match this story to government-contracting and first-award scenarios.

**Thai Pastry — National ACE**

National ACE reports support around a $250,000 recovery grant, relocation and permits, followed by a $10,000 Grubhub grant. Match this story to restaurant, capital-readiness, relocation, and grant scenarios.

**Michelle Wee — CMAA/National ACE**

CMAA reports revenue growth from $35,000 to $76,000, improved profits, and a first B2B deal. Match this story to B2B sales, consulting, and revenue-growth scenarios.

## Claim discipline

Display the source label and link with every story. Use reported outcomes as examples of CMAA support, not promises of equivalent results. Revert to `Unknown` when a current source no longer supports a claim.
